Partivia – Hotel Comparison Tool

Overview

When you open the link, you will see hotel cards with a summary of each quote: hotel name, city, star rating, price range, proposed dates, and option deadline.

  • Click any city filter at the top to narrow results (e.g. Barcelona, Madrid, etc.)
  • Click a hotel card to open the full quote details: rooms, rates, meeting rooms, F&B, terms, and contact info

Comparison Tabs

Use the icon tabs at the top to switch between views:

Budget Calculator

The Budget tab lets you estimate total costs per hotel:

  1. Set your parameters at the top: rooms, nights, participants, lunches, half-day meeting rooms, dinner price
  2. The table updates automatically with unit prices and subtotals
  3. Click any cell to override a value (highlighted in yellow)
  4. Click the VAT column to toggle between Yes / No / Unknown — totals adjust accordingly
  5. All your changes are saved in your browser automatically

Hotel Detail View

Click any hotel card to see full details:

  • Room types and rates per night
  • Meeting room specs and pricing
  • F&B options with per-person pricing
  • Terms & conditions
  • Contact info with email link

If a hotel sent multiple quote versions, you will see a badge and can expand each version.

Tips

Hover over truncated text in tables to see the full content.
Drag column borders in tables to resize columns.
Press Esc to close any popup.
Map markers are color-coded by city — check the legend in the bottom-right corner.
